RealityMedia, the digital complement to the printed book Reality Media (MIT Press 2021)

Jay David Bolter, Maria Engberg, and Blair MacIntyre

This digital version of Reality Media is a complement to the printed book. The digital version takes two forms: the web pages you see here and the 3D immersive book, which allows you to get inside AR and VR, to inhabit these new reality media. The web pages provide context for your visit as well as links into the 3D galleries. The portal () symbol indicates a link.

Creating an account and logging in

Before you can enter the immersive book itself, you can read any of the gallery pages below by clicking on the appropriate image, but you will not be able to follow the portal links into the galleries, until you create your (free) account. Creating the account and logging in are a bit complicated, but once you have done it, your browser on the current computer should remember your status. Here's what you need to do:

  • Go to this page [sign in link here] and follow the instructions to sign in
  • Complete the sign in process by checking your email for the "magic link"
  • Return to this web page to continue your visit

The gallery pages and the immersive book

You can now read this digital book in a variety of ways. You can visit the gallery pages below by clicking on the images. Each gallery page provides further portal links into that gallery. You can choose to move back and forth between the pages and the galleries or simply to move from gallery to gallery without returning to the web. If you want to enter the galleries immediately, we encourage you to start in the onboarding room , which explains features of the immersive space. If you already know how to navigate, you can enter the rotunda , which leads to all the galleries.
