The 2D and 3D sites are organized as a collection of galleries; in the 2D site, these are pages, and in the 3D site, they are 3D spaces. Each of the 2D gallery pages on this site has a corresponding immersive 3D gallery space in the immersive site. The Preface explains the concept behind the project.

You can read any of the 2D gallery pages below by clicking on the appropriate image. These pages provide context for your visit as well as links into the corresponding 3D galleries (the portal symbol indicates a link into a 3D gallery).

Creating an Account and Signing In

In order to enter the immersive 3D galleries yourself, you will need to create a (free) account and sign in. If you are not signed in, all links to the immersive Galleries will point at the sign in page.

The Gallery Pages and The Immersive Book

The 2D gallery web pages will continue to expand as the immersive site continues to be developed. While most of the immersive galleries are still being developed, you can visit one of two open immersive galleries right now:

  • the Arrival Hall, which explains features of the immersive space and provides a brief tutorial.
  • the Virtual Acropolis, which possesses a degree of digital aura, as discussed in the presence and aura page below
Other galleries will be opening soon.

The Galleries

The immersive galleries of RealityMedia are organized around a central rotunda that can be accessed from the Arrival Hall and contains an overview of each immersive gallery along with a portal to each gallery. The rotunda serves as an organizational hub and can be accessed from each of the immersive galleries.
